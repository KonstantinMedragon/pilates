“Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness. Our interpretation of physical health includes acquiring and maintaining a harmoniously developed body and a lively mind, capable of easily coping with the entire variety of daily tasks spontaneously, naturally and with pleasure.”
Joseph Pilates
Pilates. Body. Home is a reformer pilates studio where no more than three people practice at the same time. We work in mini-groups and individual sessions to keep the main focus on the body and a calm rhythm of the class. No rush, no fuss — just quality practice, deep immersion and a home‑like atmosphere.
